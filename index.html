PROJECTS

Battery Mgmt & DC Fast Charger

Lithium-ion Battery pack management system for EVs. Built & Simulated in Simulink with passive balancing system (R=36 Î© each cell). 1) Top Image: 3 Batteries in series to form a cell 2) Bottom Image: 12 cells in series to form pack. 3) Constant Current Constant Voltage (CCCV) charging profile 4) 48 min to charge from 20% to 100% SoC at 1C rate

PID Control for a Motor Module (RTOS)

Programmed in C with options to change parameters, input type (square or step), and to plot motor position on screen. 1) DLaB Library Funcitons to simplify interfacing with motor module. 2) Forward rectangular rule to perform numerical integration & backward difference rule for differentiation 3) Plot on right side is without Anti-Windup

3-Phase Fault Analysis

1) Per-unit conversion of all impedance values (using 13.8V base voltage for one of the generators to develop ETAP model) 2) Hand calculations of sub-transient fault currents 3) Tested/ simulated in ETAP with bolted fault at Bus 2 with Short-Circuit report

Function Generator

Based on LM318N op-amps. Designed using hand calculations and simulated in NI Multisim. Capabilities include: 1) Waveform selection, 2) Output amplitude control, 3) Frequency range control, and 4) Frequency control

Digital Dice

Programmed in assembly language and 'burned' onto a EEPROM microchip. 1) Push and hold button to cycle through numbers 1 to 9 extremely quickly (not visible to human eye). 2) Then release button to stop at a number (creates effect of random number selection). 3) Used 555 timer, AND gates, programmable chip and 7-segment display

Radio From Scrap Material

Features: 1) Variable capacitor made using aluminum foil and paper for tuning audio quality. 2) Coil of wire with "taps" to select frequency. Op-amp (LM741CN) was later used as diode and for amplification & improving signal quality even further.

Autonomous Fighting Robot

Used arduino nano, ultrasonic sensors, infrared sensors, and algorithms in Arduino Programming language. Robot body designed in CAD software and 3D-printed. Made to fight other robots in a sumo-style battle. 1) Detects surroundings using sensors and 2) Acts accordingly, to knock robots out of ring and WIN!
