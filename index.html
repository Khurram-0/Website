PROJECTS

Function Generator

Designed on paper using theoretical calculation and simulated in NI multisim. Capabilities include waveform selection, output amplitude control, frequency range control, and frequency control. Was a great experience building the various stages. The design & problem solving aspect (i.e. being able to make design choices and developing solutions) was the most fun.

Digital Dice

Programmed using assembly language. Push and hold button to cycle through numbers 1 to 9 extremely quickly (not visible to human eye). Then release button to stop at a number (creates effect of random number selection)

Radio

Features variable capacitor made using aluminum foil and paper for tuning audio quality. Coil with "taps" to select frequency. Most components were made from house hold items, Op-amp (LM741CN) used as diode and for amplification & improving signal quality.

Autonomous Robot

Made using arduino, ultrasonic sensors and infrared sensors (under robot). Made to fight other robots in a sumo-style battle. Detects surroundings using sensors and adapts accordingly. Robot body (purple) designed in CAD software and 3D-printed

HoverCraft

Built during University Engineering Competition. Given limited budget and time (6 hours) to design, build, and present solution to judges. Worked with a team of 4 to build a solution given the limitations (e.g. time, budget, limited number of tests). Won 3rd place out of 100 fellow Engineering students

USB Warmer

First time learning crochet. This is great for keeping USBs warm during the winter. Can also double as a finger warmer (only one finger at a time though)

Mouse Holder

Second time playing around with crochet techniques - doing random stuff. Ended up making a something that fit can fit a mouse (both kinds). Was a great learning experience - used some of these teqniques to make the laptop case later.

Crochet Laptop Case

Laptop case made using crochet techniques. Took around 1 week to complete, requiring alot of focus and patience. First time using crochet to make something I use every day! :D (great for keeping laptop screen and keyboard clean as well)
